## Interview Delivery Tips

- Speak slowly and clearly.
- Keep your introduction within 60 to 90 seconds.
- Start with your current role, then previous teaching experience, then education.
- Stress both your teaching background and computer science qualifications.
- Maintain eye contact and end with confidence.
