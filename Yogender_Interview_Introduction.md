- Stress both your teaching background and computer science qualifications.

## Interview Questions Based on Your Screenshots

These answers are written in easy to medium English so that you can speak them naturally in the interview.

---

### 1. Why do you want to leave your current job and join teaching again?

Teaching has always been close to my heart. My current role in the Haryana Government Administration Department has given me discipline, responsibility, and administrative experience, but I feel more satisfied when I interact with students and contribute to their learning. I believe my real strength and long-term interest are in teaching, especially in Computer Science.

---

### 2. Why do you want to join KVS or NVS?

KVS and NVS are respected educational institutions known for quality education, discipline, and the overall development of students. I want to work in such an environment where I can contribute as a Computer Science teacher and help students grow academically, socially, emotionally, and morally.

---

### 3. What are your strengths?

My strengths are discipline, patience, communication skills, adaptability, and sincerity toward my work. I can explain concepts in a simple manner, and my teaching experience has helped me understand different student needs and maintain a positive learning environment.

---

### 4. What is your teaching methodology?

My teaching methodology is student-centered and practical. In Computer Science, I focus on concept clarity first and then connect the topic with examples, demonstrations, and hands-on practice. I try to make learning simple, interactive, and useful for students.

---

### 5. How will you handle weak students?

First, I will try to identify their learning difficulty through interaction, observation, and regular assessment. After that, I will give them individual attention, simple explanations, extra practice, and motivation. My aim will be to improve both their understanding and confidence.

---

### 6. What programming languages do you know?

I am comfortable with Computer Science subjects and programming concepts such as C, C++, Python, and SQL. I also understand computer fundamentals and practical applications. If needed, I am always ready to learn new technologies according to curriculum requirements.

---

### 7. How will you maintain discipline in the classroom?

I believe discipline is maintained through mutual respect, clear expectations, and student engagement. I will set clear classroom rules, communicate positively with students, and keep the class active and focused. When students are engaged properly, discipline improves naturally.

---

### 8. What is the role of Computer Science in today's education?

Computer Science is very important in today's education because it develops logical thinking, problem-solving ability, digital literacy, and innovation. It prepares students for higher studies, competitive exams, and future careers in a technology-driven world.

---

### 9. What experience do you have in government schools?

I have teaching experience as a Guest Teacher in government school settings. During that period, I worked with students from different backgrounds and learned how to adjust my teaching methods according to their level and needs. That experience helped me become more practical, patient, and student-focused.

---

### 10. Why should we select you?

You should select me because I bring a useful combination of teaching experience, Computer Science background, and government work experience. My current role has made me disciplined, responsible, and organized, while my teaching experience has kept me connected with students and learning. I believe I can be a sincere and valuable member of the institution.
