### 1. Why do you want to leave your current job and join teaching again?

Teaching has always been close to my heart. My current role in the Haryana Government Administration Department has given me discipline, responsibility, and administrative experience, but I feel more satisfied when I interact with students and contribute to their learning. I believe my real strength and long-term interest are in teaching, especially in Computer Science.

---

### 2. Why do you want to join KVS or NVS?

KVS and NVS are respected educational institutions known for quality education, discipline, and the overall development of students. I want to work in such an environment where I can contribute as a Computer Science teacher and help students grow academically, socially, emotionally, and morally.

---

### 3. What are your strengths?

My strengths are discipline, patience, communication skills, adaptability, and sincerity toward my work. I can explain concepts in a simple manner, and my teaching experience has helped me understand different student needs and maintain a positive learning environment.

---

### 4. What is your teaching methodology?

My teaching methodology is student-centered and practical. In Computer Science, I focus on concept clarity first and then connect the topic with examples, demonstrations, and hands-on practice. I try to make learning simple, interactive, and useful for students.

---

### 5. How will you handle weak students?

First, I will try to identify their learning difficulty through interaction, observation, and regular assessment. After that, I will give them individual attention, simple explanations, extra practice, and motivation. My aim will be to improve both their understanding and confidence.

---

### 6. What programming languages do you know?

I am comfortable with Computer Science subjects and programming concepts such as C, C++, Python, and SQL. I also understand computer fundamentals and practical applications. If needed, I am always ready to learn new technologies according to curriculum requirements.

---

### 7. How will you maintain discipline in the classroom?

I believe discipline is maintained through mutual respect, clear expectations, and student engagement. I will set clear classroom rules, communicate positively with students, and keep the class active and focused. When students are engaged properly, discipline improves naturally.

---

### 8. What is the role of Computer Science in today's education?

Computer Science is very important in today's education because it develops logical thinking, problem-solving ability, digital literacy, and innovation. It prepares students for higher studies, competitive exams, and future careers in a technology-driven world.

---

### 9. What experience do you have in government schools?

I have teaching experience as a Guest Teacher in government school settings. During that period, I worked with students from different backgrounds and learned how to adjust my teaching methods according to their level and needs. That experience helped me become more practical, patient, and student-focused.

---

### 10. Why should we select you?

You should select me because I bring a useful combination of teaching experience, Computer Science background, and government work experience. My current role has made me disciplined, responsible, and organized, while my teaching experience has kept me connected with students and learning. I believe I can be a sincere and valuable member of the institution.

### 13. What makes you suitable for a Computer Science teacher role?

My educational background in B.Sc. Computer Science and MCA has given me a strong subject foundation. My B.Ed. has helped me understand teaching methods, and my teaching experience has shown me how to explain concepts according to student level. This combination makes me suitable for the role.

### 14. How will you make Computer Science interesting for students?

I will make Computer Science interesting by connecting concepts with daily life examples, practical activities, demonstrations, and simple projects. I will try to reduce fear of technical subjects and encourage students to learn through practice and curiosity.

---

### 15. How will you teach students with different learning levels in the same class?

I will use a balanced approach. First, I will explain the topic in a simple way for everyone. Then I will give extra support to weaker students and slightly advanced tasks to fast learners. This helps all students learn according to their level.

---

### 16. What will you do if students are not showing interest in your class?

If students are not showing interest, I will first try to understand the reason. Then I will make the lesson more interactive by using examples, questions, practical activities, and student participation. I believe interest increases when students feel connected to the topic.

---

### 17. How will you use technology in teaching?

I will use technology through smart classes, presentations, demonstrations, educational videos, and practical computer-based activities. Technology should support learning in a simple and meaningful way, not just for display.

---

### 18. How will you assess students' learning?

I will assess students through class participation, oral questions, written work, practical tasks, assignments, and periodic tests. Assessment should help us understand not only marks but also concept clarity and learning progress.

---

### 19. What will you do if a student is weak in practical work?

I will give step-by-step guidance, more hands-on practice, and simple instructions. I will encourage the student to practice basic tasks first and gradually move to more difficult work. Patience and repeated practice are important in practical subjects.

---

### 20. How will you communicate with parents about student progress?

I will communicate with parents in a respectful and clear manner. I will share both strengths and areas for improvement, and I will try to work with parents for the benefit of the student. Positive communication helps create better support for learning.

---

### 21. What is the role of discipline in education?

Discipline is very important in education because it creates a positive environment for learning. It helps students develop responsibility, self-control, respect, and regular study habits. Good discipline supports both academic and personal development.

---

### 22. How will you motivate students?

I will motivate students by appreciating their efforts, setting achievable goals, and encouraging participation. I will try to build their confidence and show them that improvement is possible with regular practice and sincere effort.

---

### 23. What qualities should a good teacher have?

A good teacher should have subject knowledge, patience, communication skills, fairness, discipline, and dedication. A teacher should also understand student needs and explain topics in a simple and effective way.

---

### 24. How will you handle a classroom with mixed behavior and different backgrounds?

I will treat all students equally and respectfully. I will create a classroom environment where every student feels included and encouraged. I will maintain discipline while also understanding individual differences in background and learning style.

---

### 25. What is your view on holistic development of students?

I believe education should support the complete development of a student. It should help students grow academically, socially, emotionally, morally, and physically. A good school and a good teacher should focus on overall personality development, not only exam marks.

---

### 26. How will you contribute to the school apart from classroom teaching?

Apart from classroom teaching, I can contribute in discipline, examination work, record maintenance, computer-related tasks, and school activities. My administrative experience can also help me support the institution in an organized and responsible way.

### 27. How do you handle pressure or responsibility?

I handle pressure by staying calm, organized, and focused on the work. My government department experience has taught me responsibility, discipline, and proper time management. I try to complete tasks sincerely and systematically.

---

### 28. What are your weaknesses?

I believe in continuous self-improvement. Sometimes I spend extra time to make sure my work is complete and correct, but over time I have learned to balance quality with time management. I always try to improve myself professionally and personally.

---

### 29. How will you deal with a student who is repeatedly disturbing the class?

First, I will speak to the student calmly and try to understand the reason for the behavior. I will counsel the student and remind them of classroom expectations. If needed, I will involve parents or school authorities in a constructive and disciplined manner.

### 30. If selected, what will be your priority as a teacher?

If selected, my priority will be to understand the students, teach sincerely, maintain discipline, and contribute positively to the school environment. I will focus on concept clarity, student participation, and the overall growth of learners.
